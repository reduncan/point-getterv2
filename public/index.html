House Point Collector

Object: Match the house point goal exactly to win. The tricky part, is doing it in the least amount of clicks as possible.

How to Play: Each round the House Point Goal will be randomly chosen. Along with the House Point Goal being randomly chosen, the amount of points per sigil is also randomly chosen. The tricky part? You will not know how many points each sigil is worth. Click a sigil to learn it's point value. Once you know the point values, it is time to match the House Point Goal exactly to win. If you go over the House Point Goal you lose.
